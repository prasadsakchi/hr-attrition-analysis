# HR Attrition Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes employee data to understand attrition patterns and identify factors associated with employee turnover.

## Business Objective
- Identify departments and roles with high attrition  
- Analyze attrition trends by age, experience, and job role  
- Support HR teams in improving retention strategies  

## Dataset
- HR employee dataset  
- Includes employee demographics, job role, department, experience, and attrition status  

## Tools Used
- Power BI (Data Analysis & Dashboard)

## KPIs Analyzed
- Total Employees  
- Attrition Count  
- Attrition Rate  
- Active Employees  

## Analysis Performed
- Attrition by department and job role  
- Attrition by age group and experience  
- Interactive dashboard for drill-down analysis  

## Key Insights
- Certain departments and job roles show higher attrition  
- Early-career employees have higher turnover  
- Attrition patterns can help HR take preventive actions  

## Key Learnings
- Using data to support HR decision-making  
- Identifying risk patterns through visual analysis  
- Importance of employee retention from a business perspective
